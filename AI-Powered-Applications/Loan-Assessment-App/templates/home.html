Tips for Loan Approval

Increase your chances of loan approval with these strategic approaches:

  • Maintain Good Credit - Regularly check and improve your credit score.
  • Stable Employment - Demonstrate consistent income history.
  • Manage Debt Ratio - Keep your debt-to-income ratio below 40%.
  • Accurate Documentation - Provide complete and truthful information.
  • Reasonable Loan Amount - Request amounts aligned with your repayment capacity.
  • Clear Loan Purpose - Have a well-defined reason for the loan request.
